# AGUI Platform - Company Knowledge Base

## About AGUI
AGUI (Advanced Graphical User Interface) is a cutting-edge platform specializing in AI-driven dynamic user interfaces. Our mission is to bridge the gap between complex AI logic and intuitive human experience.

## Refund Policy
Our refund policy is simple:
- 100% refund within the first 14 days if you are not satisfied with the platform.
- After 14 days, refunds are handled on a case-by-case basis through our support team.
- Subscriptions can be canceled at any time, but partial month refunds are not provided.

## Company History
- **2023**: Founded in San Francisco by a team of AI researchers and designers.
- **2024**: Launched our first beta of the dynamic UI engine.
- **2025**: Expanded to London and Bangalore.
- **2026**: Rebranded to INT Intelligence (Integrated Intelligence).
